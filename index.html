Six Sigma Optimization Eliminates Noble Gas Leaks

The Challenge

An Industrial Gases facility was experiencing an undocumented, recurring Argon product loss of 1.1 tons per day (TPD), leaking potential operating margins.

Strategic Intervention

Applied Lean Six Sigma DMAIC framework. Analyzed historical process variables via Minitab, performed root-cause analysis, and optimized column pressure/reflux settings.
